how to contact me

I design, build, test, launch and maintain contemporary, responsive, accessible websites. I’m proficient in HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and experience with Bootstrap, APIs, Git, JQuery, Vue.js and CMS environments such as WordPress, Shopify, and Weebly. In addition, I craft the concise, targeted marketing content critical to engaging online visitors.

If you’re interested in a project or have any questions, don’t hesitate to contact me. Email me at: laura@lauramackenzie.net.
