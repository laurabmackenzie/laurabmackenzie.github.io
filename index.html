a bit about me

Hi! I’m Laura Mackenzie, a Seattle-area web developer and content producer, and I believe every company deserves a powerful online presence. With formal training in web development from Seattle University and a strong background in writing and editing, I offer a unique blend of technical and creative skills. I design, build, test, launch and maintain contemporary, responsive, accessible websites. I’m proficient in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I have experience with Bootstrap, APIs, Git, JQuery, Vue.js and CMS environments such as WordPress, Shopify, and Weebly. In addition, I craft the concise, targeted marketing content critical to engaging online visitors.

When I think of a web designer, I typically think of a person who is knowledgeable about web development, web platforms and web design. In working with Laura, she has definitely showed her strength in these areas and also hasn’t been afraid to provide recommendations on how to enhance the user experience AND keep my site “cutting-edge”. These are all skills that I highly value from having her as my web designer. The added bonus – and somewhat of a surprise – is that Laura is also a phenomenal writer and editor. Laura ensured that all the copy on my site was crisp, clean and to the point. She is very skilled at editing my lengthy novels into just a couple of sentences – and sometimes even a phrase – to be easy to read on the web. Laura has far surpassed my expectations of a web designer and now I expect all web designers to not just be amazing with web services, but they should all be as talented with copy writing and editing as Laura is too.

Amber Wendover

Founder, Thinking People Consulting
